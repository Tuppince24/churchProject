Faith Tabernacle Family Life Center

Our Mission
Matthew 28:18-20 Exodus 9:16

Our misson is to glorify God with the worship of our lives. We gather to praise God, and respond obediently to the Great Commisson; to witness the Good News of the Kingdom of God, and made disciples of all nations. This is a place where hurting, the depressed, the frustrated, and the the confused, can find love, accrptance, healing, forgiveness, hope, help, guidance, and encouragement. A place where leaders are developedand equipped to know their purpose, potential, power, and place in the Body of Christ.

Our Vision
Matthew 4:19 Ephesians 4:13

A church whose purpose, people and approach to life is solely focused on advancing the kingdom of god. We are committed to tranforming God's people to be "fisher men"; true Disciples who walk in the power and authority of the Holy Spirit to bring unity of the faith in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ.
